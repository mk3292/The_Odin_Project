Korean Galbi

Description of Recipe:

This is a recipe for Korean beef short ribs, AKA "LA Galbi". One of the easier, tasty and quick meal option that can be made with some prep and frying. Obtained from Koreanbabsan. Purchase 3-4 lbs of sliced beef short ribs. Wash in cold water to remove bone particles and allow to drain. Grate 1/2 Korean pear, 1/2 medium onion and mix with 2 tbsp minced garlic and 1 tsp of minced ginger. If possible, blend all of these together. Then mix with 1/2 cup soy sauce, 1/2 cup water, 1/4 cup sugar, 2 tbsp honey, 1/4 cup mirin, and 2 tbsp of sesame oil. Place the short ribs into this marinade. Allow it to marinade x 3-4 hours, ideally overnight.

After it is done marinading, heat up a cast iron/bbq grill to high, and fry each short rib. Do not remove excess marinade prior to frying. Once all are fried, sprinkle sesame seeds and sliced scallions to taste.

Ingredients:

  • 3-4 lb beef short ribs
  • 1/2 grated Korean Pear
  • 1/2 grated medium onion
  • 2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp minced ginger
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 3 sliced scallions
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds

Instructions:

  1. Wash ribs w/ cold water, allow it to drain
  2. Prep ingredients and make marinade as described above
  3. Place short ribs into marinade and allow it to marinade overnight if possible
  4. Without washing off marinade, fry on high on cast iron or bbq
  5. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and scallions
