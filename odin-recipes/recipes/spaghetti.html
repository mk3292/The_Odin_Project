Spaghetti with meat sauce

Description of Recipe:

Old fashioned spaghetti with meat sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb Box of spaghetti
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1/4 grated medium onion
  • 16 fluid oz spaghetti sauce
  • 1 lb ground beef

Instructions:

  1. Bring 1 L of water to a boil
  2. Break spaghetti in half, allow to boil until al dente, 11 min
  3. While boiling, chop up onions/garlic. Under medium heat, heat up the ground beef until browned
  4. Drain beef fat/fluids once browned, add onions/garlic and fry until completely browned
  5. Add the bottle of spaghetti sauce to the meat. Leave on medium until boil, then simmer until spaghetti is ready
