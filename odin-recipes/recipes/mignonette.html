Oyster Mignonette

Description of Recipe:

Quick, easy sauce for your raw oysters.

Ingredients:

  • As many oysters as you'd like
  • 1/2 cup minced shallot
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • 1/4 cup unseasoned rice vinegar
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp sugar

Instructions:

  1. Finely mince shallot
  2. Stir in vinegars, sugar, salt
  3. Stir, then allow it to chill in refrigerator for at least 4 hrs, preferably 2 days
